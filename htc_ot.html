Overview of Occupational Therapy

Occupational Therapy (OT) helps children to participate in desired daily activities or “occupations” as independently as possible. OT helps children with physical, mental, behavioral and developmental delays.

Do you have any of these concerns?

  • Clumsy/Awkward
  • Poor concentration
  • Always moving
  • Difficulaty in crowds
  • Frequent outbursts
  • Emotional Regulation

Areas we specialize in:

  • Sensory Integration
  • Autism Spectrum
  • Attention difficulties
  • Fine motor delays
  • Visual Processing
  • Neurodevelopment Delays
