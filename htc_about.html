About Us

We provide pediatric occupational, speech and physical therapy services. We are here to not only work with your child, but to also set you up for success while at home.

What sets us apart?

We have been in business since 1998 in the Huntersville area. We began as a small Occupational Therapy clinic and branched out to include Physical Therapy in 2002. With the abundant needs in the community, we began providing Speech Therapy in the fall of 2005. We provide a true family environment, not only between our therapists, but with you as well. Our sessions are very hands on and we encourage parental participation. We will provide you with an at home plan to supplement what we do in the clinic.

Contact us today to create a plan that works for you and your child.

Empowering children and families to reach their fullest potential through movement, play, and connection.
